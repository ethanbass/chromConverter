Reads chromatograms from binary formats into R objects. Currently
supports conversion of 'Agilent ChemStation', 'Agilent MassHunter',
'Agilent OpenLab', 'Shimadzu LabSolutions', 'ThermoRaw', 'Varian
Workstation', and 'Waters Empower' files as well as various other
formats. In addition to its internal parsers, chromConverter contains
bindings to parsers in external libraries, such as 'Aston'
\<<https://github.com/bovee/aston>\>, 'Entab'
\<<https://github.com/bovee/entab>\>, 'rainbow'
\<<https://rainbow-api.readthedocs.io/>\>, and 'ThermoRawFileParser'
\<<https://github.com/compomics/ThermoRawFileParser>\>.
